5-4-3-2-1 Grounding
When anxiety spikes, this technique brings you back to the present moment.
5
Name 5 things you can see around you right now.
4
Name 4 things you can physically feel — your feet on the floor, air on your skin.
3
Name 3 things you can hear — near and far.
2
Name 2 things you can smell — or that you like the smell of.
1
Name 1 thing you can taste.
